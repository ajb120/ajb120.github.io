About Me

I'm a second-year student at the University of Colorado Boulder majoring in Creative Technology and Design with a minor in Business. Originally from Naples, Florida, I attended Phillips Exeter Academy in New Hampshire before coming to Boulder.

I'm a member of the CU women's Division I lacrosse team and play for the Israeli National Lacrosse Team. Outside of sport, I spend my time reading, flying (I am a licensed private pilot) and working on design projects that sit at the intersection of technology and human experience.

I'm focused on UI/UX design and front-end development, with an interest in building digital experiences that feel clear, considered, and genuinely useful.
